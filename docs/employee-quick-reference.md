# Shift Happens Employee Quick Reference

Last updated: 2026-03-25

## Daily Use

1. Sign in with your PIN.
2. Clock in from `/clock`.
3. Work from the active shift page at `/shift/[id]`.
4. Complete required checklist items, messages, tasks, and cleaning.
5. Enter any required drawer counts.
6. Complete safe closeout or rollover entry if your shift requires it.
7. Clock out when everything is finished.

## Clock In

- Select the correct store.
- Complete employee authentication.
- Confirm your planned start time.
- Submit clock-in.

Important:

- If you are not scheduled, the app warns that management approval will be needed.
- If you are already clocked in somewhere else, clock-in is blocked.

## During the Shift

- Acknowledge messages.
- Complete assigned tasks.
- Check off required checklist items.
- Complete or skip cleaning tasks.

Important:

- Skipped cleaning tasks need a reason.
- You cannot clock out with unfinished required items.

## Double Shifts

Double shifts require a mid-shift changeover entry.

Enter:

- X report total
- drawer count
- change count
- AM transaction count

## Clock Out

Before clocking out, make sure:

- required checklist items are done
- tasks are complete
- messages are acknowledged
- required changeover is done for double shifts

Clock-out usually requires:

- end drawer count
- change drawer count
- final confirmation checkbox

If counts are off:

- confirm the count if prompted
- mark that you notified your manager when required

## Safe Closeout

If your shift has safe closeout:

1. Enter prior X report.
2. Enter cash sales, card sales, and expenses.
3. Count bill denominations.
4. Enter drawer count.
5. Upload deposit slip photo and submit.

Important:

- Deposit slip photo is required.
- If the deposit count does not match, enter a variance reason.

## Rollover Entry

If rollover entry appears:

- enter the amount from the printed report
- submit it exactly as shown on the report

## Requests

Use `/dashboard/requests` for:

- shift swaps
- time off
- timesheet corrections
- advances

## Coverage Shift

Use `/coverage-shift/new` when you worked at another store.

Enter:

- date
- store
- time in
- time out
- optional notes

Manager approval is required before it shows on your timecard.

## Reviews

Use `/reviews` to submit Google review screenshots.

Remember:

- choose a specific store, not All Stores
- upload the screenshot first
- use a review date in the current month

## Schedule and Timecard

- `/schedule` shows upcoming scheduled shifts
- `/shifts` shows your past shifts and approved coverage hours

## Common Problems

- Session expired: sign in with PIN again.
- Cannot clock out: check messages, tasks, checklist items, cleaning, and drawer requirements.
- Time off conflict: your request may overlap with a published schedule.
- Timesheet request blocked: payroll for that period may be locked.
